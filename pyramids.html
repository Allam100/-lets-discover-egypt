Tour Overview

Enjoy this half-day trip to see the Giza pyramids and Sphinx, one of the seven wonders of the ancient world, and experience camel-riding alongside the Giza pyramids with wonderful views of the pyramids and Sahara desert.

Meet your guide in the lobby of your hotel at 9:00 AM to begin this half-day tour of the Pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx. Head toward the Pyramids of Cheops, Chephren, and Mykerinus and discover the oldest of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.

During the tour, you will enjoy a 30-minute camel ride. Your guide will help you take stunning photos to remember your trip.
